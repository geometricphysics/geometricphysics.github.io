Mathematical Notation

Algebra

$V$ linear space
$\Vector{x}$ vector
$\Field{F}$ field
$\RealField^{p,q}$ real quadratic space ($p$ for positive and $q$ for negative)
$\RealField^n = \RealField^{n,0}$$n$-dimensional real linear space
$\CliffordAlgebra_{p,q}$ Clifford algebra of $\RealField^{p,q}$
$\QuadraticForm$ quadratic form
$\CliffordAlgebra(\QuadraticForm)$ Clifford algebra of the quadratic form $\QuadraticForm; x^2 = \QuadraticForm(x)$
$\GradeInvolute{x}$ grade involute of $x \in \mathit{Cl(\QuadraticForm)}$
$\Reverse{x}$ reverse of $x \in \mathit{Cl(\QuadraticForm)}$
$\CliffordConjugate{x}$ Clifford-conjugate of $x \in \mathit{Cl(\QuadraticForm)}$

Matrices

$\mathrm{Mat}(d, \Field{F})$ $d \times d$ matrix algebra over $\Field{F}$
