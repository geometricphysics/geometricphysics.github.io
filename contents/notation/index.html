Mathematical Notation

Algebra

$U, V, W$ linear space
$\mathbb{F}$ field
$\mathbb{R}^{p,q}$ real quadratic space ($p$ for positive and $q$ for negative)
$\mathbb{R}^n = \mathbb{R}^{n,0}$$n$-dimensional real linear space
$\mathit{Cl}_{p,q}$ Clifford algebra of $\mathbb{R}^{p,q}$
$\mathit{Q}$ quadratic form
$\mathit{Cl(Q)}$ Clifford algebra of the quadratic form $\mathit{Q}; x^2 = \mathit{Q}(x)$
$\hat{x}$ grade involute of $x \in \mathit{Cl(Q)}$
$\tilde{x}$ reverse of $x \in \mathit{Cl(Q)}$
$\bar{x}$ Clifford-conjugate of $x \in \mathit{Cl(Q)}$

Matrices

$\mathrm{Mat}(d, \mathbb{F})$ $d \times d$ matrix algebra over $\mathbb{F}$
