Dirac Equation

Introduction

A review of the Pauli and Dirac matrices and algebras is given for convenience.

The following identity will be useful:

$ \begin{eqnarray} (A \otimes B)(C \otimes D) & = & AC \otimes BD \end{eqnarray} $

Notation

We will work always in natural units.

$ \begin{eqnarray} x^{\mu} & = & (x^0, x^1, x^2, x^3) = (t, x, y, z) \end{eqnarray} $

$ \begin{eqnarray} x_{\mu} & = & (x_0, x_1, x_2, x_3) = (t, -x, -y, -z) \end{eqnarray} $

$ \begin{eqnarray} \partial_{\mu} & = & \frac{\partial}{\partial x^{\mu}} \end{eqnarray} $

Pauli Matrices

$ \begin{eqnarray} I & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right],\: \sigma^{1} & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right],\: \sigma^{2} & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} 0 & -i \\ i & 0 \end{array} \right],\: \sigma^{3} & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right]. \end{eqnarray} $

Pauli Algebra

$ \begin{eqnarray} \sigma^1 \sigma^2 & = & i \sigma^3 = - \sigma^2 \sigma^1 \end{eqnarray} $

$ \begin{eqnarray} (\sigma^i)^2 & = & I \end{eqnarray} $

Spacetime Basis

$ \begin{eqnarray} \gamma^{0} & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} I & 0 \\ 0 & -I \end{array} \right] & = & I \otimes \sigma^3 \end{eqnarray} $

$ \begin{eqnarray} \gamma^{i} & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} 0 & \sigma^{i} \\ - \sigma^{i} & 0 \end{array} \right] & = & \sigma^{i} \otimes i \sigma^2 \end{eqnarray} $

Spacetime Algebra

$ \begin{eqnarray} (\gamma^{0})^2 & = & I \\ (\gamma^i)^2 & = & -I \\ \gamma^{\mu} \gamma^{\nu} & = & - \gamma^{\mu} \gamma^{\nu} \: (\mu \neq \nu) \end{eqnarray} $

The algebra can be summarized using the anti-commutator $\{\cdot,\cdot\}$

$ \begin{eqnarray} \{ \gamma^{\mu}, \gamma^{\nu} \} & = & 2 \eta^{\mu \nu} \end{eqnarray} $

where

$ \begin{eqnarray} \eta^{00} & = & 1, \\ \eta^{ii} & = & -1. \end{eqnarray} $

Dirac Matrices

$ \begin{eqnarray} \gamma^{5} & = & i \gamma^{0} \gamma^{1} \gamma^{2} \gamma^{3} & = & I \otimes \sigma^1 & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} 0 & I \\ I & 0 \end{array} \right] \end{eqnarray} $
