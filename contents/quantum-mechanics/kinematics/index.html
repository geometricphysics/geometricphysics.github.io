Quantum Kinematics

Amplitudes and Probability

The overall probability amplitude for an event $e$ is the sum of the amplitudes corresponding to every alternative $i$ that contributes to the event.

$ \begin{eqnarray*} \phi_{e} & = & \sum_{i} \phi_{i} \end{eqnarray*} $

The probability of an event is proportional to the squared norm of the overall probablity amplitude.

$ \begin{eqnarray*} p(e) & = & \frac{| \phi_{e} |^{2}}{\sum_{e} | \phi_{e} |^{2}} \end{eqnarray*} $
