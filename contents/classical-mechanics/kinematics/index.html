Kinematics

Velocity

$\vec{v} = \dot{\vec{x}} \stackrel{\mathrm{def}}{=} \Large\frac{d\vec{x}}{dt} \normalsize = \lim_{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \Large \frac{\vec{x}(t + \Delta t) - \vec{x}(t)}{\Delta t}$

Acceleration

$\vec{a} = \dot{\vec{v}} \stackrel{\mathrm{def}}{=} \Large\frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} \normalsize = \lim_{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \Large \frac{\vec{v}(t + \Delta t) - \vec{v}(t)}{\Delta t}$
