Single Particle Systems

Linear Momentum

Linear Momentum of a Particle

The linear momentum, $\vec{p}$, of a particle with mass $m$ and velocity $\vec{v}$ is defined to be

$\vec{p} = m\vec{v}$.

Angular Momentum

Angular Momentum of a Particle about a Point

The moment of momentum of a particle about a given point is called the angular momentum about that point.

If $\vec{r}$ is the displacement from the point to the particle, and $\vec{p}$ is the linear momentum of the particle, then the angular momentum, $L$, of the particle about the point is

$L = \vec{r} \wedge \vec{p}$.
