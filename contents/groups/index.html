Groups

Unitary group $U(1)$

$ \begin{eqnarray} U(1) & = & \{z \in \mathbb{C} | z \bar{z} = 1 \} \end{eqnarray} $

$ \begin{eqnarray} \cos \phi + i \sin \phi & \in & U(1) \end{eqnarray} $

Rotation group $SO(2)$

$ \begin{eqnarray} SO(2) & = & \{R \in \mathrm{Mat}(2, \mathbb{R}) | R^T R = 1, det(R) = 1 \} \end{eqnarray} $

$ \begin{eqnarray} \left[ \begin{array}{cccc} \cos \phi & - \sin \phi \\ \sin \phi & \cos \phi \end{array} \right] & \in & SO(2) \end{eqnarray} $

$Spin(2)$

$ \begin{eqnarray} Spin(2) & = & \{s \in \mathit{Cl}_2 | s \bar{s} = 1 \} \end{eqnarray} $

$ \begin{eqnarray} \cos \frac{\phi}{2} + e_{12} \sin \frac{\phi}{2} & \in & Spin(2) \end{eqnarray} $
