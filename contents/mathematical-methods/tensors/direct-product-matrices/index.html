Direct Product of Matrices

Definition

If $A$ is an $(m \times m)$ matrix and $B$ an $(n \times n)$ matrix then the direct product, $A \otimes B$, is an $(mn \times mn)$ matrix with elements

$ \begin{eqnarray} (A \otimes B)_{ik,jl} & = & A_{ij} B_{kl} \end{eqnarray} $

When constructing a direct product, the block form definition may be useful:

$ \begin{eqnarray} A \otimes B & = & \left[ \begin{array}{cccccccc} a_{11} B & a_{12} B & \cdots & a_{1n} B \\ a_{21} B & a_{22} B & \cdots & a_{2n} B \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ a_{n1} B & a_{n2} B & \cdots & a_{nn} B \end{array} \right] \end{eqnarray} $
