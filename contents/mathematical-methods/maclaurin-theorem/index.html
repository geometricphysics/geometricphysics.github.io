Maclaurin Theorem

Definition

Maclaurin's series is obtained from Taylor's series by expanding about the origin.

$ \begin{eqnarray} f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^{n}}{n!} f^{(n)}(0) \end{eqnarray} $

Application

The Maclaurin (and Taylor) series can be used to find the infinite series expansion of various transcendental functions.
