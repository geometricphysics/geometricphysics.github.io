Scalar Product

Definition of the Scalar Product

For $k$-blades $\Blade{A} = \Vector{a_1} \wedge \ldots \wedge \Vector{a_k}$ and $\Blade{B} = \Vector{b_1} \wedge \ldots \wedge \Vector{b_k}$ and scalars $\alpha$ and $\beta$, the scalar product $\ast \, \colon \, \bigwedge^{k} \RealField^{n} \times \bigwedge^{k} \RealField^{n} \rightarrow \RealField$ is defined as

$ \begin{eqnarray} \ScalarProductGA{\alpha}{\beta} & = & \alpha \beta \\ \ScalarProductGA{\Blade{A}}{\Blade{B}} & = & \left| \begin{array}{cccc} \Vector{a}_1 \cdot \Vector{b}_k & \Vector{a}_1 \cdot \Vector{b}_{k-1} & \cdots & \Vector{a}_1 \cdot \Vector{b}_1 \\ \Vector{a}_2 \cdot \Vector{b}_k & \Vector{a}_2 \cdot \Vector{b}_{k-1} & \cdots & \Vector{a}_2 \cdot \Vector{b}_1 \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \Vector{a}_k \cdot \Vector{b}_k & \Vector{a}_k \cdot \Vector{b}_{k-1} & \cdots & \Vector{a}_k \cdot \Vector{b}_1 \end{array} \right| \end{eqnarray} $

For blades of unequal grades the scalar product is zero.

Computing the Scalar Product directly using the % operator

# Computing the Scalar Product using %
from e3ga import *

a1 = VectorE3(2,0,0)
a2 = VectorE3(1,1,0)
b1 = VectorE3(0,3,0)
b2 = VectorE3(4,1,1)

A = a1 ^ a2
B = b1 ^ b2

# The Python modulo symbol is used for the scalar product.
print A % B

Identities that follow from the symmetry of the determinant

The symmetry of the determinant implies some useful symmetries in the scalar product.

$ \begin{eqnarray} \ScalarProductGA{\Blade{B}}{\Blade{A}} & = & \ScalarProductGA{\Blade{A}}{\Blade{B}} & = & \ScalarProductGA{\Reverse{\Blade{A}}}{\Reverse{\Blade{B}}} \end{eqnarray} $ 

# Demonstration of Scalar Product Identities
from e3ga import *

a1 = VectorE3(2,0,0)
a2 = VectorE3(1,1,0)
b1 = VectorE3(0,3,0)
b2 = VectorE3(4,1,1)

A = a1 ^ a2
B = b1 ^ b2

print A % B
print B % A
print ~A % ~B

The Squared Norm of a Blade

$ \begin{eqnarray} \NormSquared{\Blade{A}} & = & \ScalarProductGA{\Blade{A}}{\Reverse{\Blade{A}}} \end{eqnarray} $

The Angle between Subspaces

$ \begin{eqnarray} \cos \theta & = & \frac{\ScalarProductGA{\Blade{A}}{\Reverse{\Blade{B}}}}{\Norm{\Blade{A}} \Norm{\Blade{B}}} \end{eqnarray} $
