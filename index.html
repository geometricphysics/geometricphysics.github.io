Geometric Physics Manifesto

The purpose of this website is to research content for a Next Generation Physics Curriculum.

Principles

  • Expose the mathematical unity that is obscured by vector analysis.
  • Leverage geometric visualization of duality.
  • Provide a consolidation of Geometric Algebra/Calculus and Differential Forms/Geometry.
  • Pave the way to Maxwell, Gravitation and Quantum Mechanics.
  • Don't dumb down the Math; make it accessible instead.
  • Don't push facts and memorization. Derive theorems, even if limited in scope.
  • Geometric Algebra as the Mathematical Language foundation.
  • Calculus is essential.
  • Motivate by realistic examples and challenging problems.
  • Videos for explaining difficult concepts.
  • Modeling-based instruction
  • Programming to understand mathematical operations, function types and phenomena.
